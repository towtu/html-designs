To ensure the survival and peak performance of your hardware, adherence to these environmental and operational parameters is mandatory.

The Snapdragon® 695 processor generates heat under heavy processing loads. Never expose the chassis to direct solar radiation (e.g., car dashboards) for extended periods. If the core temperature exceeds safe limits, system throttling will occur.

> INGRESS PROTECTION

Your unit carries an IP53 rating. It is shielded against microscopic dust particles and light liquid sprays. It is NOT submersible. Complete immersion in liquids will cause catastrophic motherboard failure.
